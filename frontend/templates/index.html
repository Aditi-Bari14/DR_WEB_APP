Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

Diabetic Retinopathy is a diabetes-related eye disease that damages the blood vessels of the retina. If not detected early, it can lead to vision loss or blindness. This system assists in early detection by analyzing retinal images along with patient health information, helping clinicians identify disease severity at an early stage.

AI-Powered Detection

Uses advanced deep learning techniques to analyze retinal images and detect early signs of diabetic retinopathy with high accuracy, supporting faster and more reliable diagnosis.
